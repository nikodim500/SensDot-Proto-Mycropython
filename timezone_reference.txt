# SensDot ESP32 - Global Timezone & DST Configuration Guide

## Quick Start
1. Connect to SensDot-XXXX WiFi network
2. Open browser to 192.168.4.1
3. Choose your city from the dropdown menu
4. DST region will be automatically selected
5. Save configuration and restart device

## Supported DST Regions (Global Coverage)

### EU (Europe)
- **DST Period**: March to October
- **Rule**: Last Sunday in March to last Sunday in October
- **Covers**: UK, France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Sweden, Poland, etc.
- **Examples**: London, Paris, Berlin, Rome, Stockholm, Amsterdam

### US (North America)  
- **DST Period**: March to November
- **Rule**: Second Sunday in March to first Sunday in November
- **Covers**: USA, Canada, Mexico
- **Examples**: New York, Toronto, Los Angeles, Chicago, Mexico City

### AU (Australia/Pacific)
- **DST Period**: October to April (Southern Hemisphere)
- **Rule**: First Sunday in October to first Sunday in April
- **Covers**: Australia (except WA/NT/QLD), New Zealand
- **Examples**: Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide, Auckland

### SA (South America) - NEW!
- **DST Period**: October to March (Southern Hemisphere)
- **Rule**: Third Sunday in October to third Sunday in February/March
- **Covers**: Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Paraguay
- **Examples**: São Paulo, Buenos Aires, Santiago

### ME (Middle East) - NEW!
- **DST Period**: March to October
- **Rule**: Third Friday in March to third Friday in October
- **Covers**: Iran, Jordan, Lebanon, Syria
- **Examples**: Tehran

### AFRICA - NEW!
- **DST Period**: March to October
- **Rule**: Last Sunday in March to last Sunday in October
- **Covers**: Morocco and some North African countries
- **Examples**: Casablanca

## DST Examples by Region

### Europe (EU DST)
- **London**: Winter = UTC+0 (GMT), Summer = UTC+1 (BST)
- **Paris**: Winter = UTC+1 (CET), Summer = UTC+2 (CEST)
- **Helsinki**: Winter = UTC+2 (EET), Summer = UTC+3 (EEST)

### North America (US DST)
- **New York**: Winter = UTC-5 (EST), Summer = UTC-4 (EDT)
- **Chicago**: Winter = UTC-6 (CST), Summer = UTC-5 (CDT)
- **Los Angeles**: Winter = UTC-8 (PST), Summer = UTC-7 (PDT)

### Australia (AU DST)
- **Sydney**: Winter = UTC+10 (AEST), Summer = UTC+11 (AEDT)
- **Adelaide**: Winter = UTC+9.5 (ACST), Summer = UTC+10.5 (ACDT)
- **Perth**: Always UTC+8 (AWST) - No DST

### South America (SA DST) - NEW!
- **São Paulo**: Winter = UTC-3 (BRT), Summer = UTC-2 (BRST)
- **Buenos Aires**: Winter = UTC-3 (ART), Summer = UTC-2 (ARST)
- **Santiago**: Winter = UTC-4 (CLT), Summer = UTC-3 (CLST)

### Middle East (ME DST) - NEW!
- **Tehran**: Winter = UTC+3.5 (IRST), Summer = UTC+4.5 (IRDT)

### Africa (AFRICA DST) - NEW!
- **Casablanca**: Winter = UTC+0 (WET), Summer = UTC+1 (WEST)

## Manual Configuration

If your city isn't listed:
1. **Find nearest major city** in same timezone
2. **Set timezone offset** manually (your UTC offset)
3. **Choose DST region** or "NONE" if no DST

## Troubleshooting

**Time is wrong?**
- Check timezone offset matches your location
- Verify DST region is correct for your country
- Some regions don't use DST - select "NONE"

**DST not working?**
- Ensure device has NTP sync (check logs)
- Verify you're in a DST-enabled region
- DST calculations are automatic based on current date

**My city not listed?**
- Choose nearest city with same timezone
- Use manual configuration if needed
- Contact us to add your city in future updates

## Reset Configuration
If needed, delete `device_config.json` from device to start fresh setup.
